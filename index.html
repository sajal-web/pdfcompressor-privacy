Privacy Policy for PDF Compressor

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Thank you for using PDF Compressor ("we", "our", or "the app"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

PDF Compressor is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or files to our servers. All PDF processing (reading, compressing, saving) happens entirely on your device. We never have access to the content of your PDFs.

2. Files and Permissions

To select a PDF file, the app uses Android’s Storage Access Framework (SAF).

  • You explicitly choose which PDF to open.
  • The app reads the file only while it is being compressed.
  • The compressed PDF is saved to a location you choose (using SAF) or shared via other apps.

No file data is ever uploaded to the internet or stored on our servers.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notification (optional) – to show compression progress.
  • Internet – only if you later enable ads or crash reporting; otherwise, the app works offline.

No storage permission is required because SAF is used for all file interactions.

4. Third‑Party Services

Currently, PDF Compressor does not integrate any third‑party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services. If that changes, we will update this policy and notify you.

5. Data Security

Because all processing is local, your PDFs never leave your device. We employ Android’s built‑in security measures (sandboxing, file permissions) to protect your data.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The app is not directed at children, and all functionality requires user‑initiated file selection.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
